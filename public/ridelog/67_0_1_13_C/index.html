Saturday afternoon and filming

August 8, 2004 / Sunday

Crew: Chiaw, Drew, Patrick, Jarrod, Stephen
Mileage: 3.7 miles

The day before the ride, Chiaw and Drew came to Headquarters and started filming for a documentary on the Green Hummer. Stephen, Patrick, and Jarod were interviewed. There was going to be a ride afterwards, but time ran out. So, Chiaw and Drew came back on Saturday for a ride around town and to document our travels.

Jarrod had some business to attend to at Pinnacle Gallery. The Green Hummer dropped him and his bike off, and went to City Market.

The crew went to Vinnies, as usual. While waiting for a table, and while waiting for food to come, Chiaw and Drew filmed the crew talking to people who would stop by to look at the Hummer. Drew and Chiaw filmed a lot of footage of interaction between the crew and others.

Jarrod met up with us just as the rest of the crew had ordered, so he was able to participate in the rest of the ride.

After lunch, the Green Hummer had to go. The cameras had to be returned shortly, but it’s sometimes hard to get going. Just as you think you’re going to leave, some more curious people come by to check it out!

A sticker found on the back of a sign: it’s now on the Greeen Hummer. We’re not sure why.
