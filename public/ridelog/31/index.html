Green Hummer Fix-up Day

June 6, 2004 / Sunday

Crew: Jarrod, Danyelle, Daniel, Patrick, Nate, Stephen, Johnie, Renee

The last good day to work on the Hummer before the G8! Jarrod gets the hard-core award for staying up till 7:00 in the morning painting.

The Green Hummer gets some cool details, like a better representation of a grill, painted PVC pipes, chrome accents, a translucent hood, console, and a bicycle bell. Also, the website is improved even more, courtesy of Patrick.
