Photo shoot and Vinnie's Pizza

July 23, 2004 / Friday

Crew: Stephen, Patrick, Jarrod, Danyelle, Brian, Chiaw
Mileage: 4.0 miles

The crew started the night out, like many times before, with a trip to Vinnie’s Pizza. The wait for a table was long, so the crew relaxed for a while. The crew talked to several groups of people while waiting; some friends and some strangers.

Chiaw checked out a MiniDV camera from SCAD, and used it for the start of a mini-documentary about the Green Hummer. She was a passenger of the Green Hummer on the way to downtown from HQ, filming a good portion of the way. The Hummer makes a great camera dolly!

After dinner at Vinnie’s, the crew rode back to headquarters. It would be a busy morning for a few of the crew members, so the last hours of the ride were led by Patrick, Brian, and Stephen. The small crew spent a while shooting night photos of the Green Hummer.

The photos taken tonight were among the best photos yet of the Green Hummer. The large number of lights on the Hummer make for interesting long-exposure photos, and a slow shutter speed can make the background behind the Green Hummer blur, even at pedal-powered speed!

The streaks of light, as well as the semi-transparent nature of the Green Hummer, make beautiful photos!
