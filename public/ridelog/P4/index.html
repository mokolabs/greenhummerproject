Rollout

May 17, 2004 / Monday

Crew: Stephen, Patrick, Danyelle, Jarrod, Nate, Daniel
Mileage: 11.6 miles

Our first outing around the city! The Green Hummer was christened, and driven around town. This was the first time that many of the people who had heard about the Hummer saw it in person, and the serious rumors started.

The Green Hummer was far from finished, but it was rolling and shaped like an H2. Stephen was anxious to see what people’s responses would be, so we rode to Vinnie’s (a popular Savannah pizza spot), parked it on Franklin Square, and talked to people, or watched them from a distance. The response was amazing!
