Just a little G8 left

June 12, 2004 / Saturday

Crew: Daniel, Stephen, Danyelle, Jarrod, Patrick
Mileage: 3.2 miles

Friday was the return to normalness here in Savannah.

Everyone in the Green Hummer crew started doing mostly their own things again. The Hummer did make a scheduled appearance at DesotoRow, an art gallery in Savannah’s Starland district. The gallery was showing off some thoughtful protest art in response to the G8 Summit.

After the opening, Daniel took some of the people at the gallery on rides around the block. Then, back to Green Hummer HQ. The official end of the 2004 G8 Summit protests.

But not the end of the Green Hummer Project!
