Photo shoot

June 26, 2004 / Saturday

Crew: Stephen, Jarrod, Daniel
Mileage: 4.2 miles

The weekend was spent riding around Savannah and taking some photos for this website. There weren’t many photos of the Green Hummer by itself, so we decided to fix that!

Saturday was a relaxing day. First, we rode to lunch, as usual. We parked in front of another green Hummer on Franklin Square, but a camera was not readily available. Once a camera was available, the other green Hummer was pulling out. We missed our chance to compare two different types of green Hummers!

Afterwards, the Hummer went to the south end of Forsyth Park. Drayton Street was closed to traffic for construction, so the Hummer crew got to cruise down the busy one-way street with little worries.


Note the differences! One Hummer for earth, One Hummer for Mars!
(photo from hummer.com)

Then, the Green Hummer went off-roading, straight through Forsyth Park. Daniel took some photos of it in the green expanse of Forsyth, and the day was over.
