Getting new students' interest

September 18, 2004 / Saturday

Crew: Stephen, Jarrod
Mileage: n/a

Saturday was planned as a ride day, but scheduling problems kept an official ride from happening. Instead, Stephen and Jarrod, with Patrick’s help, made up some flyers to put on new students’ bikes to let them know about the large ride planned for next Saturday.

The two crew members went to all of SCAD’s dorms and placed flyers on every bike possible, wearing out two dollar-store staplers in the process. Luckily, there are plenty of ways to attach small flyers to a bicycle. Stephen and Jarrod now know them all.
