G8 Day One: Green Hummer Busted!

June 8, 2004 / Tuesday

Just before the start of the G8 protest parade, the Green Hummer Project was visited by the Savannah Police Department. No, we know what you’re thinking. But we really didn’t do anything. They just think the Green Hummer is pretty cool and wanted their photo taken with it.

(In fact, only one officer out of the entire SPD force has given us trouble.)
