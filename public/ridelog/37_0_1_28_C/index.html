First all-nighttime ride!

June 28, 2004 / Monday

Crew: Stephen, Danyelle, Jarrod, Daniel, Natalie, and Easy the Dog
Mileage: 3.9 miles

Sunday was part work day, part cruise night. Stephen spent the afternoon upgrading the Hummer with running lights on the top pvc support bars. At 7:30 pm, the crew set off for a quick trip around town for the first all nighttime ride.

The Green Hummer crew ate dinner in City Market, as usual. The Hummer then cruised the lively streets of Savannah.

The lights started getting dim after a few hours (with 18 lights on a car battery and no generator) and the crew called it a night, with plans for a night cruise on Fourth of July weekend.
