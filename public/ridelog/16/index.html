Friday night with a small crew

July 31, 2004 / Saturday

Crew: Chiaw, Stephen; appearances by Johnie, Renee, Natalie, and Frank the dog
Mileage: 2.7 miles

The day started with a short interview of Stephen by Chiaw and Drew. They are going to make a documentary of the Green Hummer for a class project. After the interview, Stephen and Chiaw went out to dinner with the Green Hummer.

City Market was crowded, and the wait at the usual pizza place was long. We decided to instead try to get hamburgers from the lady in the empty lot on Congress Street that we had spoken to earlier in the month. Unfortunately, still not cooking! She said that she probably won’t be grilling for a while. We heard that she’s looking for a space for a restaurant. We at the Green Hummer Project wish her the best of luck!
